About

This text is somewhat placeholder as I will be updating this site, its functionality, and the content available on it in the extreme near future (later today, and throughout the week). As a quick about, though, to those who can see this page in its current state, I am a recent undergrad graduate who now holds a BS in CS. After taking a long break from the academic/computer world (about a year) I am now reentering the field with the intention of landing a career. Obviously a bare-bones website like this might not convey that, but to be completely honest making this website and the prospect of making a LinkedIn are existential issues to me which I am overcoming. Luckily, even with such hardships, I still pieced this together. I think that even though minimalist black-and-white has been done before, it is perfectly who I am on a website like this. I will change certain positioning and add more functioning links in the future, but for now this is a wonderful placeholder representation of what I want my final page to resemble even though it is more of a draft than a final product. I do not have any pictures yet on this either, but that is because I am not groomed (Covid, life) and don't have anything recent I want to otherwise use. I will however have a regular LinkedIn profile (with picture) that will also be visible here before Blue Badge is over. I just need a little more time for all of that, which made making this website such a mental hurdle as it heavily incorporates those elements (LinkedIn, photos).
